Game Description

Yamada Game is a minimalistic avoid-em-all type game, featuring Kanji characters YAMA (山) and DA (田).

Tilt the device to move. Avoid them to survive. Grinding makes some sparks, to get a higher score. Zen to your phone. Reach the Nirvana. This is Yamada Game. Try it out.
